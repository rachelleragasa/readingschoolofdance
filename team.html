Meet The Team

Josie Norman

Principle and teacher of Reading School Of Dance. Josie started dancing at the age of 9 years old. She went on to join Impact dance in London with the amazing Hakeem Onibudo. She also studied dance full time at a dance school called The North London Dance Studios. Josie went on to dance professionally for a couple of years before setting up Reading School Of Dance in 2013. Josie is an IDTA Qualified, first aid trained dance teacher and fully trained Zumba instructor. Josie is also DBS checked.

Performances

  • Toolie Oolie Doolie for Zoonation at Trafalgar Square
  • Two’s a company for Impact Dance at Stratford Circus
  • Breakin Convention tour, for NoCo at High Wycombe - Swan Theatre
  • Performer and Co Choreographer at Big Dance Drop, The Scoop, Southbank. For Impact dance
  • Dancer at Move it- 2009, 2015, 2017, 2018
  • The Lady Legend Uk Tour
  • Many corporate shows...

Felix Clements

Felix aka FullX has been dancing for 10 years. Specialises in breakdance.

  • UK Streetdance Champion 2014 and 2016 breaking 1v1 winner
  • Bracknell's got talent 2013 winner
  • Performed as a solo performer at places like Southhill Park, the Hexagon and in front of thousands of people at Andover carnival
  • Performed for Huawei at there P20 phone launch
  • 3 x winner at UK Streetdance Championships 2015 and 2017
  • He's currently part of Streetdance Crew "RG Squad"/li>
  • DBS checked

Shaquille Mannus

Shaquille is a dancer and choreographer who has spent the last few years training and competing across Europe with the crew: Dream Chasers Collective. He has trained in various styles including Hip-Hop, Commercial, Jazz Funk, House, Afro, Dancehall, Whacking, Popping and Locking.

He’s currently training in London as part of League of Xtrodinary Dragons (LXD) under the direction of Kash Powell. His style is a culmination of everything he’s trained in and aims to elevate dancers and enable them to perform at a professional standard, without sacrificing their foundations.

Shaquille is also DBS checked.
