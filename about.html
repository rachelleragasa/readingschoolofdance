About Us

Reading School of Dance opened in April 2013. Our aim is to share our passion for dance and to support the younger generation.

RSOD specialises in mainstream Hip-Hop and street dance starting from the age of 4+ year olds. We offer a chance to be creative, build confidence, meet new friends and experience something new. We give our students amazing opportunities and we will go that extra mile for our students.

We have also been able to provide our students with a future career in dance. We have had many students go on to full dance schools and universities such as: Urdang Academy, Wilkes Academy, Creative Academy, DNA Studios, Middlelsex University, East London University, Roehampton University and East London University.
